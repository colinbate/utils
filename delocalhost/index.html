De-localhost

Tired of replacing you machine name in URLs when you are copying a localhost URL for a colleague? Just enter you machine name once here, and use this bookmarklet to provide you with your fully resolvable URL.

Takes the current page's URL and replaces localhost with whatever you specify below.

De-localhost ← Drag this to your bookmarks

Will replace:
http://localhost/my/fancy/url
with
http:///my/fancy/url
